About EPS

HOPE IS

EVRYTHING

Easy Payment System is an innovative payment solution permitted by Bangladesh Bank as a Payment System Operator (PSO). Developed by a global technology solution provider Optimum Solution and Services Ltd. (OSSL), EPS aims to provide Fintech solutions for all financial Institutions.

Customers from all financial platforms can use EPS. The aim is to serve users with the ease of instantly receiving and transferring funds. Other EPS services include Bill & fees payment, Mobile top up, and many more unique services. Our Vision is to provide a financial ecosystem to its users so that they are capable of connecting multiple accounts and manage all kinds of transactions accordingly.EPS aims to bring Easy Solution for All Transaction
